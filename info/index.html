Transport

BY CAR Parking is available at the front and to the side of the pub and also in the field adjacent at busy times. If you do come by car then a map is essential as the roads are small and the signs infrequent.

BY TRAIN/BUS/TAXI The nearest train station is Poole. From Poole you will the need to get a Number 183 Wilts and Dorset bus that goes to Blandford. I think you will likely need to get a taxi from there. Probably not a good idea then.
